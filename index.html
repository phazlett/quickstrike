Execution Controls

These controls tune how QuickStrike submits and reprices TastyTrade opening orders which might help you get filled at a better price. TastyTrade is a pay-for-order-flow (PFOF) broker, which means orders are routed to market makers for execution and the fill price is usually not what you expected.

Presets
Preset profiles. Conservative is least aggressive, Aggressive chases fastest.
Attempts
Max number of limit order submissions before stopping.
Step (¢)
Price adjustment per chase attempt.
Max Slip (¢)
Farthest allowed move from the initial submitted price.
Fill Wait (ms)
How long to wait between each submit and fill reconciliation.
Initial Price

Midpoint or natural side pricing. When Natural is selected:

  • Long orders = Initial limit order price is the Bid
  • Short orders = Initial limit order price is the Ask
Natural Offset (¢)

Used only when Initial Price is set to Natural.

  • Long orders = Bid - offset
  • Short orders = Ask + offset
